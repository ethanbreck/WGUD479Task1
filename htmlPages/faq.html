Here are some answers to commonly asked questions:

  • Power outlets are on the same standard of the United States [120 volts]

  • The drinking age is 18 and above, and alcohol is not allowewd to be served or sold between midnight and 9AM.

  • Many younger Tanitian's speak fluent english, and it is commonly spoken in more urbanized areas. Rural areas might be laking in the amount of fluent english speakers.

  • There is a hospital and a few clinics with multilingual staff.

  • Violent crime is low, but there is a chance of pickpocketing and other petty crime is possible.

  • Taniti has a large number of national holidays [listed below], where many businesses will be closed. Tourists should properly plan around the holidays.

  • Taniti uses the US dollar as its currency. Many businesses may accept Euros and Yen, but it is not mandated to accept foriegn currency. The airport and many other locations have currency exchanges. Many businesses accept major credit cards.

The list of 2024 national holidaies:

  • January 1: New Years day
  • March 5: Missionary Day
  • March 29: Good Friday
  • March 31: Easter
  • May 1: International Workers' Day
  • May 8: Victory Day
  • May 9: Ascension Day
  • May 19: Pentecost
  • May 20: Whit Monday
  • June 29: Autonomy Day
  • July 14: National Day
  • August 15: Assumption Day
  • November 1: All Saints Day
  • November 11: Armistice Day
  • December 25: Christmas Day
