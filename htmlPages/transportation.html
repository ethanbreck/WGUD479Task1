The island has a small airfield on the main island, and a harbor for ferry ships. Many people arrive via airline or cruise lines to the island.


On the main island of Taniti, there is a lot of public busses, going to the major hotels and private condos on the island. Rental cars, and a few taxis are available from multiple vendors.


Depending on where tourists aquire loding, most of the major attractions are within walking distance. There are a few neighboring islands within a 30-60 minute boat ferry ride as well, for those looking to explore the region.
